What are Sensory Toys?

Sensory Toys, also known as Fidget Toys, are toys/gadgets used to help with calming down, reducing anxiety and emotion regulation. They can (mostly) be used by people of all ages, and there are many different types, designs and sizes.


The most famous fidget toy is the Fidget Spinner, however, in my eyes, I don't count this as a sensory/fidget toy. The most popular actual Sensory Toy is the Fidget Cube, since these have sensory elements, like a switch to flip and buttons to press.
